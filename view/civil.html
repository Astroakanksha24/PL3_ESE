Civil Engineering Department

The Department of Civil Engineering was the first inchoative discipline at Walchand College of Engineering, Sangli and the oldest in Southern Maharashtra region. Starting its journey in 1947, the department has contributed in producing several eminent engineers who are in key positions of the Government organizations, public sector and infrastructure companies and have brought in significant contributions in the planning and execution of Civil Engineering projects in various regions of India.
